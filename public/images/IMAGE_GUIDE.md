# ShopSeeMe Image Guide

Replace these placeholder paths with your actual images.

## Required Images

### Hero (Full-screen, 1920x1080 recommended)
- `/images/hero/hero-1.jpg` — Main hero slide
- `/images/hero/hero-2.jpg` — Women's edit slide
- `/images/hero/hero-3.jpg` — Men's collection slide

### Categories (3:4 aspect ratio, 600x800 recommended)
- `/images/categories/men.jpg`
- `/images/categories/women.jpg`
- `/images/categories/kids.jpg`
- `/images/categories/accessories.jpg`

### Brand
- `/images/brand/statement-bg.jpg` — Brand statement parallax background

### About
- `/images/about/about-hero.jpg` — About page hero
- `/images/about/about-craft.jpg` — Craftsmanship image

### Icons (PWA)
- `/icons/icon-192.png` — 192x192 PNG
- `/icons/icon-512.png` — 512x512 PNG

### Social Sharing
- `/og-image.jpg` — 1200x630 PNG/JPG for Open Graph

## Tips
- Use Unsplash, Pexels, or your own photography
- Compress images with https://squoosh.app
- Product images are stored in Supabase Storage (uploaded via admin)
